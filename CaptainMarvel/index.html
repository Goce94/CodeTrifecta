Captain Marvel

The woman who traveled the cosmos saving lives

Here's a timeline of Captain Marvel's life:

  • 1967 - Born in Boston. She is a second child
  • 1985 - Joins the air force academy to pursue her dream of becoming a pilot.
  • 1985-89 - She rises through the ranks and becomes a captain
  • 1989 - She dies only to be reincarnated and receives her powers
  • 1995 - She found out that she was half human and half alien(Kree). She traveled to her home planet saved a lot of people.
  • 2018 - She saved the skrolls and helped them to get home. She returned to earth to help the Avangers defend the planet
  • TTD - To this day she is traveling the cosmos to help the universe
